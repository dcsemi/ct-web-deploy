About us

Our reference

We are a modern company dealing with the production of engineering units, welded steel structures and customized production in the field of small-batch production. We provide the complete processing of metal sheets, metal sections and tubes on CNC machines including welding and consequent assemblies.

All the production goes through thorough inspection and the whole production process is controlled by a modern information system.

In cooperation with our long-term partners, we also ensure all additional operations such as wet painting and powder coating.


Offshore products

A part of our production specializes in the production of special offshore containers and equipment for petroleum industry.

Cargo chasis trailers

Our second industry focus is the production of carbon steel chassis trailers.

Stainless steel products

The third, separated, part focuses on stainless steel products for various areas of usage – food, chemical and medical industries.
