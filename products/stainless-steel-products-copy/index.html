Stainless steel products

Home Our products Stainless steel products

One of the three pillars of our production is focused on welding stainless steel. Our dedicated workshop enables us to weld smaller, lighter weldments compared to the production of containers or trailers. Here, we have experience in manufacturing food industry structures, pipes, tanks, and various other welded components. In this field, we have also encountered heat-resistant stainless steels.
