Offshore products

We produce special containers and equipment intended for petroleum industry.

The containers are of the “offshore” type which means they are certified according to respective standards for reloading on an open sea.

Container tests

Necessary tests are performed using our own equipment under the supervision of certification agencies.


We perform two basic types of tests:


Production

Production test where the primary structure of the container is tested for the required load (up to 100 tons) and the container is gripped at 2 or 4 points.

Prototype test

Prototype test, in particular the fall test, where the primary structure of the prototype is dropped from a special hinge to the ground.

Cargo chasis trailers

As an engineering company, we are proud to produce a range of products that meet the needs of our customers. One of our specialties is the production of cargo chassis trailers, which are essential for transporting goods across the country. Our cargo chassis trailers are made with high-quality carbon steel, which ensures their durability and strength.

Our production processes

At our engineering company, we use only the highest quality carbon steel in the production of our cargo chassis trailers. Our skilled engineers and technicians work diligently to ensure that every step of the production process is carried out to the highest standards. We use state-of-the-art equipment to cut, shape, and weld the steel, ensuring that each trailer is built to last.


Our cargo chassis trailers are designed with the needs of our customers in mind. They are made with high-quality carbon steel, which ensures their strength and durability. Our trailers are also designed to be lightweight, making them easier to transport and reducing fuel consumption. Additionally, we offer a range of customization options, allowing our customers to tailor their trailers to their specific needs.


Conclusion

At our engineering company, we are committed to producing high-quality cargo chassis trailers that meet the needs of our customers. Our use of high-quality carbon steel ensures that our trailers are durable, strong, and long-lasting. If you are in need of a cargo chassis trailer, look no further than our engineering company.
