Offshore products

We produce special containers and equipment intended for petroleum industry.

The containers are of the “offshore” type which means they are certified according to respective standards for reloading on an open sea.

Container tests

Necessary tests are performed using our own equipment under the supervision of certification agencies.


We perform two basic types of tests:


Production

Production test where the primary structure of the container is tested for the required load (up to 100 tons) and the container is gripped at 2 or 4 points.

Prototype test

Prototype test, in particular the fall test, where the primary structure of the prototype is dropped from a special hinge to the ground.

Engineering

We offer our customers the production from simple burnouts on our laser cutting machine including bending, welding and surface treatment, up to the delivery of complicated weldment units including machining and consequent assembly.

We are able to weld steel structures up to the weight of 5000 kg.

Stainless steel products

We have the best quality welding and metal-working machines in the field of the stainless steel production. We emphasize the quality of workmanship. By using our laser cutting machine and CNC press brake, we can offer producing precise metal sheet parts and engineering units for various industrial sectors.
