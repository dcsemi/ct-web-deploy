Company history

The company was established in the region with a long-lasting tradition in engineering production. We draw on many years of experience of the company owners and key workers in the field of engineering and production, including welding, which has lasted for more than 10 years.

Important milestones in the company history:

2002

Stainless steel

The production of stainless steel machines and equipment started

2007

Offshore containers

The production of offshore containers and equipment for petroleum industry started

2012

ISO 9001:2008

The company was awarded certification according to ISO 9001:2008

2013

Laser cutting machine

The company bought a new laser cutting machine and a press brake

2014

Information system

The company implemented a new DIALOG control system

2015

CNC machines

The company bought a new CNC vertical machining center and CNC turning machine

2017

ISO 3834-2

We were awarded certification ISO 3834-2

2019

ERP System

Update of our ERP system to DIALOG 3000S

2021

Cargo trailer chassis

Focus on carbon steel welding of cargo chassis

2022

Long welds automatization

Development of new automatization of welding long welds

2023

New 2D laser cutting machine

Company acquires high power and more dimensional laser cutting machine

Modern company dealing with the production of engineering units, welded steel structures and customized production in the field of small-batch production.
