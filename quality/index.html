Quality policy

CT-Products s.r.o. realizes that stable high quality of its products and services can only be achieved through a quality policy that applies to all company activities and all employees. Meeting the needs of our customers and exceeding their expectations is an integral part of daily work and presentation of the quality principles of our company. The following quality policy has been adopted to fulfill these principles. ...

  • The company's basic orientation is to become a sought-after business partner and supplier in the production of offshore containers, including assembly, the production of engineering units, and the production of stainless steel machines, equipment, and tanks according to customer specifications, creating a sense of security not only for the customer but also for the company's employees.
  • The company management fully identifies with the requirements of the ČSN EN ISO 9000 series standards, which means effective implementation principles and continuous improvement of the management of processes that affect production quality in the company, including fulfilling relevant requirements relating to the company's activities.
  • Company management is constantly creating the necessary organizational, personnel, and financial resources to implement, develop, and improve the quality management system.
  • The permanent goal is to continuously improve our activities and ensure the quality of the products and services we produce. Our subcontractors play a significant role in the production of our products, so we will develop continuous and active cooperation with them in clarifying mutual requirements and their implementation.
  • We will create appropriate conditions for the education of company employees in the production of offshore containers, including assembly, the production of engineering units, and the production of stainless steel machines, equipment, and tanks according to customer specifications.

ISO 9001

Production quality requirements have been continuously increasing. Our company therefore underwent successful certification of the quality management system according to ISO:9001:2015.
